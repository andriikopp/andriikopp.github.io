  • Business Process Management (focused on process modeling and models quality analysis, BPMN, IDEF0, EPC, DFD)
  • Architecture (models storage and processing, ArchiMate, TOGAF)
  • Business Intelligence (analytical dashboards design problems, MS Power BI)
  • Databases (business rules translation into relational schema, SQL, MySQL, MS SQL Server)
  • and related topics of Information Technology
